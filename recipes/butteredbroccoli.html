Buttered Broccoli recipe!

Here's my simple recipe for Buttered Broccoli, and it's absolutely delicious!
It's a great dish for everyone, especially those following the keto diet and to
make it, you only need a few basic spices, butter, and of course, broccoli.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Broccoli
  • Stick butter
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder

The Six Easy Steps:

  1. Go ahead and pour some of the frozen Broccoli into a microwave safe bowl
  2. Pour some water into the bowl, the amount varies with how much broccoli you'll be making, use around three tablespoons to start out
  3. Cover the bowl with a microwave safe plate or something similar
  4. Put it in the microwave for about 5 minutes, however watch it closely! as cooking time varies with the power of your microwave and too long will burn it.
  5. Take it out after its nice and fork tender and throw in about 1 tablespoon of butter and mix
  6. Now that the butter is melted and is coating the broccoli now throw in your seasoning to taste and mix

Done!

There you have it, all done and it only took around 20 minutes if that!
