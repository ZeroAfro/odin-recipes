Yogurt Bark Recipe!

This is a great and "quick" treat that you can prep in as little as
just ten minutes and keep frozen even longer! It stays good in the freezer for quite
a while and is good to pull out whenever you feel the urge to snack!

Ingredients:

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Granola

The Four easy Steps:

  1. Mix three cups of greek yogurt and 1/4th a cup of honey together in a bowl
  2. Pour this mixture into a parchment lined baking pan
  3. Add any other desired toppings and then freeze overnight or until it's frozen solid
  4. Break the now hardened yogurt bark into however many pieces you would like

Done!

Feel free to serve them now or you can put them in a sealed container to store in the freezer
just don't leave them out as they can soften and get messy. These are very good for you and feel
free to tweak it to reduce the calories or stuff them with more depending on which you need!
