Air Fryer Chili Dog Recipe!

These air fryer chili dogs will absolutely leave you craving more. They
are decently fast to cook and gives the the amazing taste of a grilled hotdog
without all that work on a grill! With a optional step for those who like slightly
cooked buns as well.

Ingredients:

  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Hot Dogs
  • Onions
  • Chili
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

The Six Easy Steps:

  1. Take the hot dogs and cut slits about one inch apart that are around 1/4th a centimeter deep
  2. Now use your Air Fryer to cook them at 400F for five minutes
  3. While thats cooking go ahead and heat the chili however you like
  4. Dice your Onions
  5. Now that your hot dogs are done you can either turn the Air Fryer off or you can place the hot dog in a bun and cook for one more minute
  6. Top the hot dog with the chili from the beginning
  7. Throw on the shredded cheese and onions
  8. Now heat in the microwave so just about ten seconds

Done!

Now you can enjoy the best hot dogs that only takes a fraction of the tim!
